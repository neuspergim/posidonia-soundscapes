# Methods

This directory documents how acoustic data were collected, calibrated,
processed, and quality-checked. Method records should be detailed enough for
another researcher to understand the measurement chain and reproduce the
analysis where access to equivalent data is available.

## Documentation checklist

- study area, habitat, deployment dates, and sampling design;
- recorder, hydrophone, serial number, firmware, and clock settings;
- sample rate, bit depth, duty cycle, channels, and gain;
- hydrophone sensitivity and calibration source/date;
- deployment geometry, depth, mounting, and environmental conditions;
- time zone and clock-drift corrections;
- preprocessing, filters, integration windows, and acoustic reference units;
- quality-control criteria and excluded data;
- software, toolbox, and script versions.

Clearly distinguish laboratory calibration, manufacturer specifications, and
empirical field-comparison corrections.
